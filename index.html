Hi there! I’m Amelia, one of the top-25 travel vloggers according to The New York Times rating with more than 5kk subscribers on my Youtube channel.

As I'm flying around the world as a stewardess, I’m telling about the most spectacular places in my videos. Usually, I have no more than 2 days in one city, so I have a special format of video guides named #2daystravel. I provide my subscribers with up-to-date information about the best places of interest suited to every fancy.

How did I start blogging?

Ever since I was a kid, I liked reading and writing. But when I joined the Fly Dubai crew, I slowed down because I simply couldn’t find the time! I missed reading, so I went looking for something to read besides my safety instructions. Besides, I lacked info on the cities we were flying to, so every time I happened to be in some new country, I didn't know where to go and what to see first. I ended up subscribing to 24Travel, and a whole new blogging world opened up to me! First, I started to read and watch videos from other vloggers, then I left comments, and finally, I wondered: why not start vlogging myself?

So in August 2014, I launched my Youtube channel TravelWithAmelia and Instagram! Vlogging is now a part of my life and I can't go without it anymore.

My vlog covers journeys to different cities of the world, with honest reviews on hotels, beaches, museums and much more. You can watch long and short videos literally about every place in the world. Of course, they are a little bit judgmental, but I'm trying to show both the good and bad sides of each place. My audience is almost everyone, who is fond of traveling or is just going to start his first journey.
